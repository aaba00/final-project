environmental justice + local law 97

mapping large multifamily residential buildings, relative 2021 emissions, and nyc ej areas

click the buttons at the top of the window to view the buildings.

passed in 2019, local law 97 mandates emissions reductions for most buiidlings in new york city that are 25,000 sq ft or over. almost 70% of these buildings are mutlifamily residential complexes.

while reducing emissions benefits the environment, it is key to ensure that environmental justice communities (defined as communities that are more vulnerable to potential environmental injustices) do not bear undue costs from building retrofits.

this map provides a starting point for analysis by mapping residential buildings covered by local law 97 and their relative emissions. emissions are approximated by energy use intensity, which measures the amount of energy a buidlings uses per square foot.
